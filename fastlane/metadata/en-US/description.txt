Buzz is every college event, on one map.

Live discovery for parties, clubs, sports, free food, study sessions, and academic talks happening tonight on and around your campus. Tap an event, RSVP in one second, see which friends are going.

ONE APP FOR COLLEGE LIFE
• Live map of every event happening tonight, within walking distance
• Join clubs in a tap — badges show on your profile (Member, Officer, President)
• Free food alerts (the killer push notification every student wants)
• Paper-free: clubs replace stacks of printed flyers with one QR poster
• Streaks that keep track of how many events you've shown up to
• See which friends are going to what — kills the "what's happening rn" group text

FOR CLUB OFFICERS
• Forward any event email to @events.buzz.app and we draft it for you
• Recurring events, templates, duplicate from last week — 10-second posting
• Bulk invite members by CSV or paste
• Broadcast push + email to your roster (rate-limited — no spam)
• Check-in QR at the door, real attendance vs RSVPs
• Tabling Mode: one iPad at your booth broadcasts your club to every nearby phone
• Real analytics: RSVPs → attendance funnel, best time to host, paper saved

BUILT FOR EVERY COLLEGE JOURNEY
